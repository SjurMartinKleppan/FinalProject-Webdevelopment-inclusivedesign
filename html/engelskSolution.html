Our solution

Our solution is an app that logs the user and households emission. It measures electricity and water consumption,
which means of transport you use when traveling, measure how much garbage the user throws away.
It can also compare the user's consumption with the national average and at what times the consumption is the greatest.
This way, you as a consumer can know if you have an unnecessarily high consumption of reasources and eventually get tips on how to lower this.
